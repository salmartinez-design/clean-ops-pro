SQL for this report:
  SELECT 
    DATE_TRUNC('day', scheduled_time) as date,
    COUNT(*) as job_count,
    SUM(amount) as revenue,
    AVG(amount) as avg_job_value,
    SUM(allowed_hours) as allowed_hours,
    SUM(actual_hours) as actual_hours
  FROM jobs
  WHERE company_id = $1
    AND status = 'complete'
    AND scheduled_time BETWEEN $2 AND $3
  GROUP BY DATE_TRUNC('day', scheduled_time)
  ORDER BY date ASC

  Employee | Days Worked | Job Hours | 
  Clock Hours | Base Pay | Tips | 
  Additional Pay | Overtime | Gross Pay

  Overtime = any hours over 40 in the week
  Calculated at 1.5x rate automatically

TOTALS ROW at bottom (bold, bg #F7F6F3)

EXPORT BUTTONS:
  "Export for ADP" → CSV formatted for ADP import
  "Export for Paychex" → CSV formatted for Paychex
  "Export for Gusto" → CSV formatted for Gusto
  "Export PDF" → clean formatted PDF report

ADP CSV format:
  Company Code, File #, Reg Hours, O/T Hours,
  Earnings 3 Code, Earnings 3 Amount,
  Earnings 4 Code, Earnings 4 Amount

Paychex CSV format:  
  Employee ID, Hours Worked, OT Hours,
  Earnings Code, Earnings Amount

Gusto CSV format:
  Employee Email, Regular Hours, OT Hours,
  Bonus, Reimbursement

SQL for payroll:
  SELECT 
    u.id, u.first_name, u.last_name,
    COUNT(DISTINCT DATE(t.clock_in_at)) as days_worked,
    SUM(t.actual_hours) as job_hours,
    SUM(EXTRACT(EPOCH FROM 
      (t.clock_out_at - t.clock_in_at))/3600) as clock_hours,
    u.pay_rate,
    SUM(ap.amount) FILTER (WHERE ap.type = 'tip') as tips,
    SUM(ap.amount) FILTER (WHERE ap.type != 'tip') as additional_pay
  FROM users u
  LEFT JOIN time_clock_entries t ON t.user_id = u.id
  LEFT JOIN additional_pay ap ON ap.user_id = u.id
  WHERE u.company_id = $1
    AND t.clock_in_at BETWEEN $2 AND $3
  GROUP BY u.id, u.first_name, u.last_name, u.pay_rate
