A few things worth flagging:

The light theme will make your tenant color system look dramatically better — a company's brand blue on white feels premium, while that same blue on near-black looks washed out. PHES blue (`#0065A5`) on a white sidebar will look sharp and professional.

The preset color swatches are a nice touch for new tenants who don't know their hex code — they just click their closest match during onboarding.

The discounts system has two key design decisions baked in. The `display_for` field is the important one — office-only discounts are for your admin staff to apply manually to invoices, online discounts are what clients see when they self-book. You can create a "staff discount" that clients never see, or a public promo code that anyone can use. That flexibility is what MaidCentral has and it's genuinely useful.
