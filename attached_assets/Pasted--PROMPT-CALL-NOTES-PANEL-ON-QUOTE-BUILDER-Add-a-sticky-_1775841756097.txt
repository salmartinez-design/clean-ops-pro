---

**PROMPT — CALL NOTES PANEL ON QUOTE BUILDER**

Add a sticky notes panel to the right side of the internal quote builder that is visible on every step of the quote. Notes persist through all steps and can be manually pushed to the job notes when the quote is converted.

---

**STEP 1 — DATABASE**

```sql
ALTER TABLE quotes 
  ADD COLUMN IF NOT EXISTS call_notes text;

ALTER TABLE jobs
  ADD COLUMN IF NOT EXISTS office_notes text;
```

`call_notes` on quotes stores everything typed during the call. `office_notes` on jobs is the destination when notes are pushed.

---

**STEP 2 — STICKY NOTES PANEL ON QUOTE BUILDER**

On the internal quote builder (`/quotes/new` and `/quotes/[id]/edit`), add a sticky notes panel on the right side of the layout. The panel must remain visible and in the same position on every step of the quote — Customer Info, Property Details, Service & Pricing, Add-ons & Notes, review.

**Panel design:**

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│  Call Notes                 │
│  ─────────────────────────  │
│  [textarea — full height]   │
│                             │
│                             │
│                             │
│  Last saved: just now       │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

- Panel width: 280px on desktop, hidden on mobile (mobile gets a floating note button instead — see Step 3)
- Background: `#FFFFFF`
- Border: `#E5E2DC`
- Header: "Call Notes" in secondary text color `#6B6860`, small caps style
- Textarea: full height of the panel, no resize handle, `font-size: 14px`, `line-height: 1.6`, `padding: 12px`
- Placeholder text: "Type notes as you speak with the client..."
- Auto-save: debounce 2 seconds after last keystroke — save `call_notes` to the quote record via PATCH `/api/quotes/[id]/notes`
- Show "Last saved: just now" or "Last saved: X min ago" below the textarea
- Notes persist if the office navigates between steps — the textarea content never resets

---

**STEP 3 — MOBILE FLOATING NOTE BUTTON**

On mobile, the sidebar is hidden. Show a floating circular button in the bottom right corner (above the bottom nav) with a pencil icon. Tapping it opens a full-screen modal with the same textarea. Saving closes the modal and returns to the quote step.

---

**STEP 4 — PUSH NOTES TO JOB**

On the quote detail page and on the "Save & Convert to Job" confirmation screen, add a **Push Notes to Job** button below the call notes textarea:

```
[Push Notes to Job]
```

- Button style: secondary outline button, not primary
- When clicked, show a confirmation modal:

```
Push Notes to Job

The following notes will be added to the job office notes:

[preview of call_notes text — editable in this modal]

[Cancel]  [Confirm & Push]
```

- The text in the modal is editable — office can trim or modify before pushing
- On "Confirm & Push": write the (possibly edited) text to `jobs.office_notes` for the job linked to this quote
- Show a success toast: "Notes added to job"
- If the job doesn't exist yet (quote not yet converted), show: "Convert this quote to a job first, then push notes."
- After pushing, the call_notes on the quote remain unchanged — pushing copies, does not move

---

**STEP 5 — NOTES ON QUOTE DETAIL PAGE**

On the read-only quote detail page, show the call notes in a section labeled "Call Notes" below the quote line items. Same panel style as the builder. Editable inline — office can update notes on a saved quote at any time. Auto-saves on blur.

---

**STEP 6 — NOTES ON JOB DETAIL PAGE**

On the job detail page, show `office_notes` in a section labeled "Office Notes" visible to Owner and Office roles only. Editable inline. Never visible to technicians.
