SECTION 7 — Scorecard System
Inbound SMS webhook:
	∙	Twilio inbound webhook at /api/sms/inbound
	∙	On receipt: match sender phone number to most recent job_completed SMS sent to that number within last 48 hours
	∙	Parse reply: accept 1, 2, 3, or 4 only
	∙	Invalid reply → send back: “Please reply with 1, 2, 3, or 4 to rate your cleaning.”
	∙	Valid reply → write to scorecard table, trigger scoring logic
Score weights:
	∙	4 = 100%
	∙	3 = 75%
	∙	2 = 40%
	∙	1 = 0%
Scorecard table: id, company_id, job_id, client_id, employee_ids[], rating, weight, excluded, excluded_reason, excluded_by, created_at
Score calculation:
	∙	Rolling weighted average of all non-excluded ratings for each technician
	∙	Formula: sum of weights ÷ number of rated jobs × 100 = score %
	∙	Example: ratings of 4, 3, 4, 2 → (100 + 75 + 100 + 40) ÷ 4 = 78.75%
	∙	Score applies equally to all techs assigned to that job
	∙	Excluded ratings are completely removed from calculation
95% floor:
When any tech’s score drops below 95%:

	∙	Send SMS to the tech: “Hi {{first_name}}, your recent client feedback score has dropped below our standard. Please connect with the office team.”
	∙	Create a ticket in the office ticket queue: “{{tech_name}} scorecard below 95% — current score: {{score}}%. Review recommended.”
	∙	Office can flag any individual rating as excluded with a reason (client unreasonable, disputed, error) — excluded ratings are removed from score retroactively
