## THE NEW MARK

The logo consists of:
1. A solid mint green circle (#00C9A0) containing a bold 
   white Q
2. Three shine burst lines radiating from the LEFT side 
   of the circle in mint green (#00C9A0) — upper-left 
   diagonal, left horizontal, lower-left diagonal
3. The wordmark "leno" sitting immediately to the right 
   of the circle with tight spacing

The shine lines should be:
- Upper-left: from about 10 o'clock position, angling 
  up-left at roughly 45 degrees, length ~8px, 
  stroke-width 2.5, stroke-linecap round
- Left: perfectly horizontal pointing left from 9 o'clock 
  position, same weight
- Lower-left: from about 8 o'clock position, angling 
  down-left, same weight

## QLENOMARK.JSX

The standalone icon mark — used for favicon and 
collapsed sidebar states.

Renders as a rounded square (rx proportional to size) 
with mint green (#00C9A0) fill. Inside: three white 
shine lines on the left side, bold white Q on the right 
side of center.

Props: size (default 32). The rounded square fills the 
full size. Q is centered slightly right to balance 
the shine lines.

---

## QLENOLOGO.JSX

The full horizontal lockup — mark + wordmark.

Props:
- size: 'sm' | 'md' | 'lg' (default 'md')
- theme: 'light' | 'dark' (default 'light')
- layout: 'horizontal' | 'stacked' (default 'horizontal')

Size values:
- sm: circle r=10, font-size 14px, total height 28px
- md: circle r=14, font-size 18px, total height 36px  
- lg: circle r=18, font-size 22px, total height 44px

Horizontal layout:
- Mint circle with white Q and left-side shine burst
- "leno" wordmark immediately right, tight spacing (4px gap)
- Wordmark color: #0F1117 on light theme, #FFFFFF on dark
- Font: Plus Jakarta Sans, weight 600, letter-spacing -0.5px

Stacked layout:
- Mark centered on top
- "QLENO" centered below in Plus Jakarta Sans weight 600,
  letter-spacing 2px
- Color follows theme
